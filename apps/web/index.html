Preserving Indigenous Languages

Uqalimaaq is dedicated to the revitalization and preservation of Pacific Northwest and Alaskan Indigenous languages. Our platform uses the sacred symbol of the totem pole to guide learners through progressive language mastery, honoring the cultural significance of these languages while making them accessible to new generations.
